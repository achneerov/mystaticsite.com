How to get your Spotify data:

  1. Go to Spotify Privacy Settings
  2. Scroll down to "Download your data"
  3. Request "Extended streaming history" (this can take up to 30 days)
  4. Download the ZIP file when ready
  5. Upload it here to see your stats!

Your data is processed entirely in your browser - nothing is uploaded to any server!
